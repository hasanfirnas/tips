eclipse - shortcuts
===================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<Shift>+<Ctrl>+<Up>   - Go to previous function, method or class
<Shift>+<Ctrl>+<Down> - Go to next function, method or class
<Ctrl>+</>            - Toggle comment
<Ctrl>+<f>            - Search for text in source
<Ctrl>+<k>            - Find again
<Ctrl>+<s>            - Save
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
