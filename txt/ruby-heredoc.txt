ruby - heredoc or multi-line string
===================================

Simple heredoc :

[source, ruby]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
name = 'Louis'

text = <<EOT
#{name}
was
here
EOT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

With indent :

[source, ruby]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
text = <<-EOT.gsub(/^ {2}/, '')
  Louis
  was
  here
EOT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Additional tag : heredocument
